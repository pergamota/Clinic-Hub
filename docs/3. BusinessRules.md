# ClinicHub - Business Rules

## BR01 - Unique Email
Each user must have a unique email address.

---

## BR02 - Unique CPF
Each patient must have a unique CPF.

---

## BR03 - Unique CRM
Each doctor must have a unique CRM.

--- 

## BR04 - Unique Specialty
The system must not allow duplicate specialty names.

---

## BR05 - Appointment Date
Appointments cannot be scheduled for dates in the past.

---

## BR06 - Appointment Status
An appointment must have one of the following statuses:
- SCHEDULED
- COMPLETED
- CANCELLED

---

## BR07 - Medical Record
A medical record can only be created for completed appointments.

---

## BR08 - Medical Record Uniqueness
Each appointment can have only one medical record.

---

## BR09 - Exams
One appointment may have multiple exams.

---

## BR10 - User Role
A user must have exactly one role:
- ADMIN
- DOCTOR
- PATIENT

---

## BR11 - Authentication
Only authenticated users can access the system.

---

## BR12 - Doctor Specialty
Every doctor must be associated with one specialty.

---

## BR13 - Appointment Ownership
A patient can only view their own appointments.

---

## BR14 - Medical Record Access
Only the assigned doctor can create or update a medical record.

---

## BR15 - Exam Access
Patients can only view exams related to their own appointments.

---

## BR16 - Role Immutability
A user's role cannot be changed after creation. If a person needs
a different role, a new User account must be created.
